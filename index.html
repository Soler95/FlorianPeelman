About
Florian Peelman

Florian Peelman is a violist known for his warm, expressive sound and his ability to bring depth and subtle nuance to both solo and chamber music repertoire.

His teaching methods are said to help students succeed in highly competitive orchestral auditions, with some going on to secure positions in leading professional orchestras around the world.
